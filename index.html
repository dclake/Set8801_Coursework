Do you know Saint Lucia?

This website intends to be an interactive learning tool that will increase the general awareness and knowledge of the Caribbean Island nation of Saint Lucia and use a quiz to accomplish this aim. The website is therefore aimed primarily at tourists and visitors to the island. It may be later extended to encourage travel to the island by offering promotions or coupons to high-scoring visitors but will also be marketed to locals to promote national pride..
